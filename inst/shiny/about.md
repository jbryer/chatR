### About

This application was created by [Jason Bryer](mailto:jason.bryer@cuny.edu), Ph.D. for his [DATA 606 Statistics and Probability](http://data606.net) course.

Report issues here: https://github.com/jbryer/chatR/issues

### Data Use Statement

This application saves all chat queries for the purpose of studying how students use chatbots for completing assignments and to evaluate the accuracy of results. Your use of this chatbot will in now way effect your grade.
